Returns & Exchanges Policy

Returns for new, unused products are never a problem. Items must be returned in their original packaging, with tags attached. If a return is received and cannot be re-sold as a new item, the package will be returned to you. Returns must be received within 30 days after purchase for a full refund. Anything past 30 days, a gift certificate will be issued. Once a return is received by LAX.com, it can take up to 10 business days to process the return. Returns are not processed on Saturdays & Sundays. LAX.com is not responsible for shipping charges on returned items that we filled correctly and we do not refund the original shipping costs associated with your original order.

Shoe/Cleat Return Policy

Shoes and cleats must be returned in their original packaging for re-sale purposes. The branded shoe box must be placed in another box to prevent damage. Do not tape the branded shoe box closed or adhere shipping labels to the original branded shoe box.
If shoes are worn in any manner, on turf, or grass they are not acceptable as a return.

Helmets/Final Sale Items

Custom items, like stringing, cannot be returned. Cascade helmets have a $35 restocking fee, if returned. STX Rival and Warrior custom helmets are a final sale and cannot be returned.

Mouthguards

Lax.com will not accept open, tried for fit, or used mouthguards for return.

Exchange Policy

Lax.com does not offer an exchange policy.
We suggest you follow the return policy for items you wish to exchange and place a new order for your desired items.
